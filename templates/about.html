About ThermoSim

ThermoSim is an interactive tool for exploring the fundamental principles of thermodynamics, created by Ferdinand.

My Mission

As the creator of ThermoSim, my mission is to provide an interactive and educational tool for simulating fundamental thermodynamic processes. I aim to make complex concepts more accessible and intuitive for students, educators, and professionals. By visualizing relationships between pressure, volume, and temperature, users can gain a deeper understanding of the principles that govern energy conversion and heat transfer.

The Science Behind ThermoSim

ThermoSim allows you to explore key thermodynamic processes based on foundational laws:

Ideal Gas Law

Simulations are based on the ideal gas law (PV=nRT), describing the state of a hypothetical ideal gas. This serves as a foundational model for understanding the behavior of real gases under different conditions.

First Law of Thermodynamics

ThermoSim visualizes energy conservation: the change in internal energy of a system equals the heat added minus the work done by the system (Î”U = Q - W).

  • Isochoric (Constant Volume): Work done is zero as volume doesn't change.
  • Isobaric (Constant Pressure): Work is done on or by the system as volume changes.
  • Isothermal (Constant Temperature): The internal energy of an ideal gas remains constant.
  • Adiabatic: No heat or matter is exchanged between the system and its surroundings.

Technology & Development

ThermoSim is built with modern frontend technologies, including HTML5 and Tailwind CSS, and powered by a FastAPI backend to handle complex thermodynamic calculations. This open-source project reflects my dedication to creating high-quality educational tools.
